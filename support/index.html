Common questions

How do I get started?

Open the app, complete the short tour, then add a child profile. You can use Family, Med Box, and Pickup on this device without a server account.

How do I delete my account / data?

Tap your avatar → Edit profile → Delete account. You can also remove the app from your device to clear on-device app data.

Is this medical advice?

No. Med Box tools are references only. Ask a licensed clinician for medical decisions.

Why does the app ask for location?

Pickup routes and weekend weather need location or a place you enter. You can deny permission; some features will be limited.
